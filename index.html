About Me

Andrew Loverde is a Costume Crafter turned Coder. He has studied Computer Science at Fayetteville State University. He also was previously enlisted by the United States Air Force as an Aircraft Structural Maintainer. Andrew lives in Eastern Texas with his partner and their pet cat and dog.

Andrew's love of Fantasy and Horror brought him into the world of Anime and Game Costume creation. He and his spouse have competed in multiple costume contests internationally. Some awards include judge favorite at Yeticon and Galaxycon Raleigh.

In 2016, Andrew decided to take his creative problem-solving into Computer Science through Fayetteville State University, then again with a Coding Boot Camp through the University of Minnesota in 2023. He believes these investments will lead to a fruitful career in coding.

When not crafting or coding, Andrew enjoys Dungeons & Dragons, cooking, and playing in local Pokemon Trading Card Tournaments. For up-to-date info, Check out their crafting live stream, SanpyGames, on Twitch.tv.
